Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 15, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to MyTikTokAnalytics ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application that integrates with TikTok.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information from TikTok

When you authenticate with TikTok Login Kit, we may receive:

  • Your TikTok username and display name
  • Your TikTok profile picture
  • Your TikTok open ID (unique identifier)
  • Basic profile information you authorize us to access

2.2 Information We Store

We store the following information:

  • Access tokens (securely encrypted)
  • Your TikTok open ID
  • Information necessary to provide our services

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Authenticate you with TikTok
  • Provide analytics and insights about your TikTok content
  • Improve our services
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure token storage with encryption
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to personal information

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With your explicit consent

6. TikTok's Privacy Policy

Your use of TikTok is also governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review it.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke access to your TikTok account at any time
  • Opt out of data collection

8. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide our services. You may request deletion at any time by revoking app access in your TikTok settings.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for users who are at least 13 years old (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect information from children under this age.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
